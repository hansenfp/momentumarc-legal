# Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

## Introduction

Momentum Arc ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the Momentum Arc mobile application collects, uses, and protects information.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information

If you choose to sign in using Google or Apple, we may collect:

* Email address
* Display name
* Account identifier

This information is used solely to provide account functionality and cloud synchronization features.

### Recovery Data

Momentum Arc may store information you choose to track, including:

* Recovery systems
* Progress statistics
* Milestones
* App preferences
* Subscription status

Recovery data is never sold or shared with advertisers.

### Analytics and Diagnostics

To improve the app, we may collect anonymous usage information, including:

* Feature usage
* App performance metrics
* Crash reports
* Device platform and app version

We use trusted service providers such as Firebase, RevenueCat, PostHog, and Sentry.

## Cloud Storage

Cloud backup and synchronization are optional features.

When enabled, your data may be securely stored using cloud infrastructure operated by trusted providers.

## How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

* Provide app functionality
* Synchronize data across devices
* Process subscriptions
* Improve stability and performance
* Provide customer support

## Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information.

Information may be shared only with service providers required to operate the application, including:

* Google Firebase
* Apple
* Google Play
* RevenueCat
* PostHog
* Sentry

## Data Retention

Data is retained for as long as necessary to provide the service or comply with legal obligations.

Users may request account deletion where applicable.

## Children's Privacy

Momentum Arc is not intended for children under 13 years of age.

## Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect user information. However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

## Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
